VERSION 1.1.2

New update features
- trains for next 20 hours for planning
- alarm before arrival for sleepy commuters

By the customer request train schedule is extended up to next 20 hours. It is useful for planning purposes. Status of the trains is updated automatically along with remaining time for next two choo-choos. Departed ones became gray and remain for reference. Update button rebuilds the schedule and cleans up departed trains.

Another new great feature is alarm. Set alarm to wake you up 5 minutes before arrival. It is possible to set alarm for future and en-route trains only. Once on the train feel free to take a nap without risk of waking up at Speonk or even further.
