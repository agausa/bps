Round World

“Round World” is a reference application with comprehensive information on present day countries around the world. It’s virtual collection contains an accurate location of 30,000 cities, country’s boundaries and up to date statistics.

Feature includes:
- World factbook
- Globe view to locate a country on the globe
- locate country by name
- country’s flag
- zoom in/ zoom out
- locate any city by name
- day/night overlay mode
- latitude-longitude grid

This is essential reference about our modern World. Everything fits in the small package and does not require Internet connection.

Fit the World in your pocket!
